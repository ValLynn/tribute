Bessie Coleman

Lady of the Sky

Who Was Bessie Coleman

Bessie Coleman was an American aviator and the first Indigenous (Cherokee or Choctaw) and African-American woman to earn a pilot license.

Timeline Highlights

  • 1892 - Born in Atlanta, Texas to George and Susan Coleman; raised in Waxahachie, Texas.
  • 1994 - The Coleman Family moved Moved to Chicago
  • 1915 - Moved to Chicago at 23 years old
  • 1916 - Worked in Cosmetology and as a Manicurist, winning a contest for the best and fastest manicurist in Chaicago, Illinois.
  • 1917 - Married Claude Glenn, a friend of her brother, Walter.
